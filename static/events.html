She Hacks 2015

About the event

Girl Geek Sydney are running our second all female hackathon - SheHacks - which will see up to 100 female developers, designers, UX and producers working in small groups to come up with digital ideas and prototypes.

This year the theme is 'Environment and Education'.

The event runs over two days:

Friday 8 May from 6:30pm - Midnight. "and" Saturday 9 may from 9am-6pm .
At Google HQ, Pirrama Rd, Pyrmont.
